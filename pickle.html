Our Story

Khozy Pickle began in a small home kitchen with a passion for creating delicious, traditional pickles. Our founder, Mrs. Khozy, started pickling vegetables using her grandmother's cherished recipes, sharing them with friends and family.

What started as a hobby soon blossomed into a thriving business as word spread about our exceptional flavors. Today, we continue to honor those traditional methods while using only the highest quality, fresh ingredients.
